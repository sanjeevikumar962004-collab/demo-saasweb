Common questions

Quick answers before you reach out.

How fast do you actually respond to support tickets?

+

Our median first-response time is 47 minutes during business hours (Mon–Fri, 8 AM–6 PM in your local time zone). Enterprise plan customers get a dedicated Slack channel with a 15-minute SLA. All plans receive 24/7 critical-incident support.

Can I book a live demo before I sign up?

+

Absolutely. Select "Sales enquiry" in the form above and mention you want a demo. A solutions engineer will reach out to schedule a 30-minute session tailored to your specific use case. No sales pressure — just a real working demo of your workflow.

Do you offer custom enterprise pricing?

+

Yes. For teams of 50+ or organizations with specific compliance, volume, or on-premise requirements, we build a custom package. Reach out via the sales form and we'll put together a proposal within 24 hours.

I'm a journalist writing about AI automation. Who do I contact?

+

Email press@stackly.io directly or use the form with "Press / Media" selected. We have a full media kit with product screenshots, executive bios, and fact sheets ready to send. We respond to press enquiries within 4 hours.

I'm interested in a technology or reseller partnership.

+

We have an active partner program for consultancies, agencies, and technology platforms. Select "Partnership" in the form and describe what you're building. Our partnerships team runs a structured evaluation process and moves quickly.
